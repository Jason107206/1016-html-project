History

Founded in 2006, our clinic has served the Hong Kong community for four decades, evolving from a small local practice into a comprehensive center for family wellness.

Over the years, we have witnessed generations of families grow, providing a consistent anchor of medical reliability amidst the city’s rapid transformation.

This deep-rooted history allows us to blend time-honored patient relationships with the latest clinical advancements, ensuring that every individual who walks through our doors benefits from both our extensive experience and our unwavering commitment to modern, evidence-based care.

  • 2006: Our clinic has founded.

  • 2012: Our clinic has moved from Arran Street, Mong Kok.

  • 2015: Dr. Sze Yat Sum Yuki has joined our clinic.

  • 2018: Dr. Chan Sze In has joined our clinic.
